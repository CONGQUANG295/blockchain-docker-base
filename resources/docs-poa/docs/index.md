## What is ${Name Chain}?

${Name Chain} is an innovative solution to bring programmability and interoperability to ${Name Chain}. ${Name Chain} relies on a system of PoA consensus that can support short block time and lower fees. The most bonded validator candidates of staking will become validators and produce blocks. The double-sign detection and other slashing logic guarantee security, stability, and chain finality.

Please read the [FAQ](./faq/${name}/general.md) to get started.

You can:

- Send and receive ${SYMBOL}

network for the assets



**For developers**, you can also:

${Name Chain}, ${Name} boasts smart contract functionality and compatibility with the Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM). The design goal here was to leave the high throughput of ${Name Chain} intact while introducing smart contracts into its ecosystem.

Because ${Name} is EVM-compatible, it launched with support for the rich universe of [Ethereum](https://academy.${domainchain}/en/articles/what-is-ethereum) tools and DApps. In theory, this makes it easy for developers to port their projects over from Ethereum. ## ${SYMBOL} (and Other Coins) MainNet Switch

${Name Coin} (${SYMBOL}) was an ERC20 token on the Ethereum network.<br/>
After the launch of ${Name Chain}, ${Name Coin} (${SYMBOL}) is being converted into native ${SYMBOL} tokens on the main network via the exchange platform at [${domainchain}](https://www.${domainchain}), a pragmatic and efficient way to perform the initial token swap.

${Name Chain} is ready for other projects to migrate their tokens to take advantage of performant transactions with more liquidity options and native marketplace features.<br/>
More information about how projects can set themselves up for this (via [${domainchain}](https://www.${domainchain}) or partners) will come soon.
